<p><strong>The Low Noise Synthesizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frequency Modulation</li><li>Phase Modulation</li><li>Pulse Modulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Noise Synthesizer Market consists of three major types: Frequency Modulation (FM), Phase Modulation (PM), and Pulse Modulation (PM). FM synthesizers modify the frequency of a carrier wave to generate different tones. PM synthesizers alter the phase of the carrier wave to create diverse sounds. PM synthesizers manipulate the width and position of pulses in a carrier wave to produce various rhythms. These three types offer musicians and sound designers a wide range of options for creating unique and high-quality audio.</p></p>
