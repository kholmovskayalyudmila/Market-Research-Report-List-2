In the active Q-switch market, the Q-switch is integrated into the laser cavity itself, allowing for faster switching times and higher pulse power. On the other hand, passive Q-switches are separate devices that can be easily added to existing laser systems, providing a cost-effective solution. Both types of Q-switches play an essential role in industrial applications by improving laser performance and enabling precise laser processing.</p></p>
