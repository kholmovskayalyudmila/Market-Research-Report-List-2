<p><p>The semiconductor chip testing tool market can be categorized into four types. Nondestructive testing tools assess the quality of chips without causing any damage, ensuring their reliability. External inspection devices examine the external features and physical condition of chips. Electrical testing tools measure voltage and current in chips to ensure proper functioning. Fault testing tools identify any faults or defects in chips, enabling their rectification. Overall, these tools play a vital role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor chips.</p></p>
