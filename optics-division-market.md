<p><p>The Optics Division market encompasses various types of products, including lenses, prisms, diffraction gratings, diaphragms, superpolished optics, and others. Lenses are optical devices used for focusing or dispersing light. Prisms, on the other hand, help in redirecting and manipulating light beams. Diffraction gratings aid in separating light into different wavelengths. Diaphragms control the amount of light passing through an optical system. Superpolished optics undergo additional polishing processes for enhanced precision. Lastly, the "others" category pertains to additional optical products like mirrors, filters, and laser components. These various products cater to diverse applications in industries such as healthcare, aerospace, telecommunications, and research.</p></p>
