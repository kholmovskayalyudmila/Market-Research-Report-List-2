<p><p>The Radio Frequency Coaxial Cable market is classified into two types based on impedance: 50Ω and 75Ω. The 50Ω coaxial cable is primarily used in high-frequency applications such as telecommunication, aerospace, and military sectors. It offers better signal transmission, making it suitable for long-distance data transmission. On the other hand, the 75Ω coaxial cable is commonly used in audio/video applications like cable television, CCTV systems, and broadband internet services. It provides improved signal quality and low signal loss, making it ideal for shorter distance transmissions within buildings or homes.</p></p>
