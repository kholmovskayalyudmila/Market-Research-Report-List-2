<p><p>MgO wallboards are available in three main types based on their thickness. Thin MgO wallboards have a thickness below 8 mm, while medium ones have a thickness ranging from 8 to 15 mm. On the other hand, thick MgO wallboards have a thickness above 15 mm. These three types cater to different construction needs and applications. Thin boards are suitable for areas where space is limited or lightweight construction is desired, while medium and thick boards offer increased strength and durability for projects requiring higher load-bearing capabilities or enhanced fire resistance.</p></p>
