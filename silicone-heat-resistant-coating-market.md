<p><p>The silicone heat resistant coating market includes three main types: primer, topcoat, and middle layer. Primers are the initial layers applied to surfaces to enhance adhesion and create a uniform base. Topcoats are the final layers added for protection, durability, and aesthetics. They provide a glossy finish and resist external elements like UV rays and chemicals. Middle layers are applied between the primer and topcoat to provide additional heat resistance and insulation. These layers ensure long-lasting performance and improve the overall effectiveness of the coating system.</p></p>
