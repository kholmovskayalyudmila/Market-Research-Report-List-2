<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical isolators are devices used to disconnect power supply from a circuit for maintenance or safety purposes. There are different types of isolators available in the market. Single break isolators have a single contact point, while double break isolators have two contact points, enhancing their reliability. Pantograph type isolators use mechanical linkages to open and close the circuit. These isolators are important components in electrical systems, ensuring safe operations and efficient maintenance.</p></p>
