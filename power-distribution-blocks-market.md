<p><p>Power distribution blocks are key components in electrical systems that enable the distribution of power from a primary source to multiple secondary sources. There are three types of power distribution blocks based on the number of primary connections per pole. In the 1 primary connection/pole market, the block allows only one primary connection to be made per pole. In the 2 primary connections/pole market, two primary connections can be made per pole, enabling more flexibility. Lastly, in the 3 primary connections/pole market, three primary connections are possible per pole, offering even greater distribution capabilities.</p></p>
