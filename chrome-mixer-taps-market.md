They are commonly used for smaller sinks or basins. 2-hole taps have separate holes for the spout and the handle, allowing for more flexibility in installation. 3-hole taps have separate holes for the spout and two handles, usually used in larger sinks. Other market types may include wall-mounted taps or freestanding taps, offering different installation options to suit different bathroom or kitchen setups.</p></p>
