<p><p>PoE security cameras are available in different types including bullet, dome, and PTZ. Bullet-type cameras are cylindrical in shape and are usually installed outdoors. They are known for their long-range viewing capabilities. Dome-type cameras are compact and discreet, making them suitable for indoor use. They offer a wide-angle view and can be easily mounted on ceilings. PTZ cameras, on the other hand, provide pan, tilt, and zoom functions remotely. They are versatile and can be controlled to focus on specific areas.</p></p>
