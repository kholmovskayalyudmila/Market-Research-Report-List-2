<p><p>There are three types of Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) foam packaging in the market: metal, conductive, and dissipative polymer. Metal foam packaging is made from metal, providing excellent protection against ESD. Conductive foam packaging is infused with conductive carbon or metal fibers, allowing it to quickly dissipate static charges. Dissipative polymer foam packaging, on the other hand, has a higher resistance to static charges, as it is made from polymers infused with static-dissipative agents. All three types aim to effectively prevent damage caused by electrostatic discharges during transportation and storage.</p></p>
