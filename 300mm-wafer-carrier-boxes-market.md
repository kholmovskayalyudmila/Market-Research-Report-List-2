<p><strong>The 300mm Wafer Carrier Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB)</li><li>Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 300mm wafer carrier boxes market comprises two types: Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB) and Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP). FOSB is a container used for the transportation and storage of 300mm wafers. It features a front opening lid for easy access to the wafers. FOUP, on the other hand, is a standardized version of FOSB and is predominantly used in automated wafer-handling systems. It provides secure protection, contamination control, and efficient handling of wafers during transport or storage. Both FOSB and FOUP serve crucial roles in the 300mm wafer supply chain.</p></p>
