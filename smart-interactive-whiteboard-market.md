<p><p>Smart Interactive Whiteboard Market Types can be classified into two categories: Fixed Type and Portable Type. </p><p>Fixed Type refers to whiteboards that are permanently installed in a specific location, such as classrooms or conference rooms. These boards are often larger in size and offer more advanced features, such as touch sensitivity and wireless connectivity.</p><p>On the other hand, Portable Type whiteboards are designed to be easily transported and set up in different locations. They are smaller in size and usually come with a stand or wheels for easy mobility. These boards are suitable for professionals who frequently move between meeting rooms or for educators who need flexibility in their teaching environments.</p></p>
