<p><p>Bidirectional TVS diodes are a type of transient voltage suppressor that provides protection against voltage spikes in both directions. There are two main market types for Bidirectional TVS diodes: axial and radial. </p><p>Axial Bidirectional TVS Diodes have leads at both ends and are designed for through-hole mounting, making them suitable for applications with high power and high voltage requirements. </p><p>Radial Bidirectional TVS Diodes, on the other hand, have leads that are located on one side and are primarily designed for surface mounting. These diodes are smaller in size and are suitable for applications where space is limited.</p></p>
