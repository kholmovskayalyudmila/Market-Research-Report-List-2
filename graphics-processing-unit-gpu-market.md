<p><strong>The Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Independent GPU</li><li>Integrated GPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) market consists of two main types: Independent GPU and Integrated GPU. Independent GPUs are standalone graphics cards that can be installed separately in a system, providing high-performance graphics capabilities for tasks like gaming and video editing. Integrated GPUs, on the other hand, are built-in graphics processors within the computer's central processing unit (CPU). These provide basic graphics functionality suitable for everyday tasks like web browsing and office applications. Both independent and integrated GPUs cater to different consumer needs and price ranges in the market.</p></p>
