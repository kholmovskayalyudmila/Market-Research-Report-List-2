<p><p>The thermally conductive graphite gasket market can be classified into three types: single layer high thermal conductivity graphite film, composite high thermal conductivity graphite film, and multilayer high thermal conductivity graphite film. </p><p>Single layer high thermal conductivity graphite film refers to a thin graphite film with excellent thermal conductivity properties that allows for efficient heat transfer. </p><p>Composite high thermal conductivity graphite film is a combination of graphite and other materials, such as metal or ceramic, to enhance thermal conductivity and improve overall performance. </p><p>Multilayer high thermal conductivity graphite film consists of multiple layers of graphite films stacked together to increase thermal conductivity and provide better heat dissipation.</p></p>
