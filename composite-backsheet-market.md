<p><strong>The Composite Backsheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluoropolymer</li><li>Non-Fluoropolymer</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Composite Backsheet Market can be classified into two types: Fluoropolymer and Non-Fluoropolymer. Fluoropolymer backsheet is made of materials like polyvinyl fluoride (PVF) or polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF), offering excellent resistance to UV, moisture, and corrosion. These backsheet types are commonly used in high-end applications. On the other hand, Non-Fluoropolymer backsheet is made of materials like polyethylene terephthalate (PET) or polyamide (PA), providing a cost-effective solution for general applications. These backsheet types are known for their flexible and lightweight nature, making them suitable for various solar panel installations.</p></p>
