<p><strong>The Photolithography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV (i-line)</li><li>DUV (KrF, ArF Dry, ArFi)</li><li>EUV</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Photolithography equipment is used in the semiconductor industry to imprint circuit patterns onto silicon wafers. UV (i-line) equipment utilizes ultraviolet light to create these patterns, while DUV (KrF, ArF Dry, ArFi) machines use deep ultraviolet light for higher resolution. EUV equipment, on the other hand, employs extreme ultraviolet light, enabling even finer circuitry. These different market types cater to the diverse needs of semiconductor manufacturers, who require equipment capable of producing precise and complex circuit patterns to meet the demands of advanced technologies.</p></p>
