<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Textural food ingredients market can be categorized into different types. The first type is hydrocolloids, which includes substances like carrageenan and agar that provide texture and stability to food products. The second type is starch and derivatives, such as modified starches, which are used as thickeners and stabilizers. Emulsifiers are another type, examples being lecithin and mono- and diglycerides, which help in creating and stabilizing emulsions. Lastly, the "others" category includes various ingredients like proteins, fibers, and gums, which also contribute to the desired texture and stability in food products.</p></p>
