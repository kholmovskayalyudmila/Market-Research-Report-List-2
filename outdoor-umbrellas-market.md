<p><p>The outdoor umbrellas market comprises three main types: centre pole umbrellas, offset pole umbrellas, and wall-mounted umbrellas. Centre pole umbrellas are supported by a single pole in the center, providing shade in an upward direction. Offset pole umbrellas have a side pole, allowing for flexible positioning and enabling shade to be placed precisely where needed. Wall-mounted umbrellas are attached to a wall or vertical surface, maximizing space while offering shade from above. These variations cater to different preferences and outdoor settings, providing options for optimal sun protection.</p></p>
