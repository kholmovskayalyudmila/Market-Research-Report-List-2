<p><p>Terry knitting machines are used for producing fabrics with piled loops. There are two main types of terry knitting machine markets: Single Pile Loop and Double Face Loop. Single Pile Loop machines are capable of creating fabrics with a single pile loop, resulting in a softer feel and lower fabric thickness. On the other hand, Double Face Loop machines can produce fabrics with two pile loops, resulting in a plush, thicker fabric. These different market types cater to the varying requirements and preferences of customers in the textile industry.</p></p>
