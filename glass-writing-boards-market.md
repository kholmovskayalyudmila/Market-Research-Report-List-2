<p><p>Glass writing boards are available in two types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic glass boards have a metallic layer that allows the use of magnets to attach notes, documents, or other materials. This feature makes them ideal for collaborative workspaces, offices, or classrooms where users can easily display and organize information. On the other hand, non-magnetic glass boards lack the metallic layer and do not provide the magnet attachment capability. Despite this limitation, they still offer a sleek and modern alternative to traditional whiteboards, suitable for writing or drawing with dry erase markers.</p></p>
