The increasing popularity of gourmet cooking and the demand for freshly ground spices are key factors driving market growth. People are increasingly opting for whole peppercorns instead of pre-ground pepper due to their higher quality and better taste.</p><p>Latest Trends:</p><p>One of the latest trends in the manual pepper mill market is the use of innovative materials in the construction of pepper mills.
