<p><p>The ultrawide monitors market includes two main types: curved surface and flat surface monitors. Curved surface monitors have a slight bend to their display, which enhances the viewing experience by enveloping the user's peripheral vision. This design aims to create a more immersive experience and reduce eye strain. On the other hand, flat surface monitors have a traditional flat screen display without any curve. These monitors are more commonly used for professional work or gaming, providing a wide field of view with sharp and accurate visuals.</p></p>
