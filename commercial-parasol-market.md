<p><p>The commercial parasol market consists of three main types: centre pole parasols, cantilever parasols, and wall-mounted parasols. Centre pole parasols are the most common type, with a central pole supporting the canopy. Cantilever parasols have an off-center mast, allowing for flexible positioning and greater shading options. Wall-mounted parasols are attached to a wall or structure, providing shade while saving space. These market types cater to different needs and preferences, offering various options for commercial establishments looking to provide outdoor shade and comfort to their customers.</p></p>
