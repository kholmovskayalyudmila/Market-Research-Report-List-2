<p><p>The waterproof portable Bluetooth speaker market can be classified into three types: AC/DC Bluetooth speakers, AC Bluetooth speakers, and DC Bluetooth speakers. AC/DC Bluetooth speakers can operate on both alternating current (AC) power supply and direct current (DC) power supply, offering flexibility for use indoors and outdoors.
