<p><p>The inflatable dome tents market can be classified into two types: frame inflatable dome tents and integral inflatable dome tents. Frame inflatable dome tents are designed with separate inflatable tubes that form the frame structure, providing stability and support. On the other hand, integral inflatable dome tents have an integrated inflatable structure that forms both the frame and the tent walls. Both types of tents offer easy and quick setup, versatility in terms of size and configuration, as well as portability, making them popular choices for camping, outdoor events, and temporary shelters.</p></p>
