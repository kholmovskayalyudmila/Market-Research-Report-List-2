<p><p>Woodcare paint brushes are available in three main types: flat, round, and roller. </p><p>Flat brushes have straight bristles that provide even coverage and are ideal for large, flat surfaces. They work well for applying varnishes, stains, and paints.</p><p>Round brushes have rounded bristle tips and are excellent for detail work and edging. They allow for precise control and are commonly used for furniture painting or intricate designs.</p><p>Roller brushes have a cylindrical shape with a foam or fabric cover. They are best suited for covering large areas quickly and efficiently, making them ideal for painting walls or floors.</p></p>
