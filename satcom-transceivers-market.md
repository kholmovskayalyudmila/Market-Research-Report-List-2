<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>SATCOM transceivers are communication devices used to transmit and receive signals in satellite communication systems. C Band refers to a frequency range of 4 to 8 GHz, suitable for long-distance coverage with large antennas. Ka Band operates at higher frequencies, typically between 26.5 to 40 GHz, providing higher data rates but limited coverage. Ku Band (12 to 18 GHz) offers a balance between coverage and data rates. L Band (1 to 2 GHz) offers good penetration through vegetation and is used for mobile applications. X Band (8 to 12 GHz) provides high-quality signals but with limited coverage range.</p></p>
