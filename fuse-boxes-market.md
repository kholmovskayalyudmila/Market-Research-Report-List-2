<p><p>The Fuse Box market comprises various types including Cartridge Fuse Panels, Aluminum Wiring Electrical Panels, and Breaker Panels. Cartridge Fuse Panels are designed to protect circuits from overloads and short circuits by housing fuses. Aluminum Wiring Electrical Panels are specific panels used for electrical systems with aluminum wiring, providing safety precautions due to the special considerations associated with this type of wiring. Breaker Panels entail a more modern approach, utilizing circuit breakers instead of fuses to control and protect electrical circuits.</p></p>
