<p><p>Edible halal gelatin is available in the market in two types - acid hydrolysis type and alkaline hydrolysis type. The acid hydrolysis type of gelatin is produced by subjecting the raw materials to an acidic environment, which breaks down the proteins into smaller peptides. On the other hand, alkaline hydrolysis type gelatin is produced by subjecting the raw materials to an alkaline environment. Both types of gelatin have specific applications and differences in properties, making them suitable for various food products while adhering to halal dietary requirements.</p></p>
