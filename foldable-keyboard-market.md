<p><p>The foldable keyboard market offers two types of connections: Bluetooth and USB. It eliminates the need for physical cables and allows for easy portability. On the other hand, USB connection involves connecting the keyboard to a device using a USB cable. Ultimately, both types cater to different user preferences and needs.</p></p>
