<p><p>Anti-blue Ray Myopia Lenses are designed to protect the eyes from harmful blue light emitted by electronic devices, preventing myopia (nearsightedness). There are two main types of lenses available in the market - Spherical and Aspheric. Spherical lenses have a uniform curvature and are most commonly used. They effectively correct nearsightedness but may cause distortions in peripheral vision. Aspheric lenses, on the other hand, have varying curvatures across the surface, providing enhanced visual clarity and reducing peripheral distortions. They are particularly suitable for individuals with high prescriptions or those seeking a more natural vision experience.</p></p>
