<p><p>Aircraft elastomers refer to the type of materials used in the aviation industry for various applications like seals, gaskets, O-rings, and more. The market for aircraft elastomers can be divided into four main types: EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), Silicone, Fluoroelastomers, and Others. EPDM offers excellent resistance to weathering and temperature, while Silicone elastomers have superior flexibility and low-temperature resistance. Fluoroelastomers, on the other hand, exhibit exceptional chemical resistance. The "Others" category includes elastomers like nitrile rubber, neoprene, and butadiene rubber, which have their own unique properties and applications in aircraft manufacturing.</p></p>
