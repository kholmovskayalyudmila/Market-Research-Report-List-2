<p><p>Clamp Ampere Meters are devices used to measure electric current without disconnecting the circuit. There are two main types in the market: Transformer Type and Electromagnetic System. </p><p>Transformer Type Clamp Ampere Meters operate based on the principle of transformer action, where the current flowing through the conductor induces a voltage in the secondary winding, allowing measurement.</p><p>On the other hand, Electromagnetic System Clamp Ampere Meters function by using an electromagnetic field to calculate the current passing through the conductor, offering accurate measurements.</p><p>Both types cater to the needs of different applications, providing options for users based on their requirements and preferences.</p></p>
