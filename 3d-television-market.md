<p><p>3D television refers to a display technology that provides viewers with a perception of depth by presenting stereoscopic images. <p><p>3D television market types can be broadly categorized into non-glass free and glass-free technologies. Non-glass free 3D TVs require viewers to wear specialized 3D glasses to perceive the three-dimensional effect. These glasses help separate the images displayed on the screen for each eye, resulting in a 3D visual experience. On the other hand, glass-free 3D TVs utilize advanced display technologies to create a 3D effect without the need for glasses. This is achieved through the use of screen filters or lenticular lenses that direct different images to each eye, allowing for a glasses-free 3D viewing experience.</p></p>
