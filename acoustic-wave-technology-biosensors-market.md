<p><p>Acoustic Wave Technology Biosensors are used in various applications for detecting and measuring parameters such as mass, viscosity, and elasticity of materials. The Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor is based on the principle that changes in mass on the crystal surface result in frequency changes, allowing for precise measurements. On the other hand, Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor utilizes the interaction between surface waves and analytes for detection, enabling high sensitivity. Both types of biosensors find use in sectors like healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food industry for their accurate and rapid detection capabilities.</p></p>
