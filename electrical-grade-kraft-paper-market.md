<p><p>Electrical Grade Kraft Paper is classified into two main types: Plain Kraft Paper and Creped Kraft Paper. </p><p>Plain Kraft Paper is a smooth, untextured paper that offers high insulation properties, making it suitable for electrical applications. It is commonly used in transformers, cables, and other electrical equipment.</p><p>On the other hand, Creped Kraft Paper is a textured paper with a wrinkled surface. It provides better flexibility and higher tensile strength, which makes it suitable for applications that require bending and folding, such as in capacitors and motors.</p><p>Both types serve as essential insulation materials in the electrical industry, offering distinct characteristics to meet different application requirements.</p></p>
