<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>PC VR headsets come in three types based on their motion tracking capabilities. 3DOF (degrees of freedom) headsets offer basic rotational tracking, allowing users to look around but not move within the virtual space. 6DOF headsets provide both rotational and positional tracking, enabling users to move freely within a limited space. On the other hand, 9DOF headsets offer not only rotational and positional tracking but also additional features like hand and body tracking, resulting in a more immersive virtual reality experience. Each type caters to different needs and budgets, offering varying levels of interaction within the virtual environment.</p></p>
