<p><p>The wine corks market consists of two main types: natural corks and synthetic corks. Natural corks are made from the bark of cork oak trees and are considered traditional and high-quality closures for wine bottles. They allow a small amount of oxygen to enter the bottle, facilitating aging and maturation processes. On the other hand, synthetic corks are typically made from plastics and offer consistency in quality, preventing cork taint, which affects the taste and aroma of the wine. While natural corks have a long-standing reputation, synthetic corks provide a more affordable and reliable alternative.</p></p>
