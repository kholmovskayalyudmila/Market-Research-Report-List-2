<p><p>The child safety helmet market can be classified into three types: full face helmet, open face helmet, and half helmet. Full face helmets cover the entire head, providing maximum protection to the child's head, face, and chin. Open face helmets cover the top and sides of the head but leave the face exposed, offering moderate protection. Half helmets cover only the top portion of the head, providing limited protection. These different types cater to various preferences and safety needs of parents and children.</p></p>
