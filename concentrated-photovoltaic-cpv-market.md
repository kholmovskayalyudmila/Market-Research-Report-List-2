<p><p>The Concentrated Photovoltaic (CPV) market is divided into two main types: Low Concentrated Photovoltaic (LCPV) and High Concentrated Photovoltaic (HCPV). LCPV systems use optics to concentrate sunlight onto smaller, more efficient solar cells, enhancing their electricity generation. They are often used in utility-scale applications. On the other hand, HCPV systems concentrate sunlight onto highly efficient multi-junction solar cells which are more expensive but yield higher energy efficiency. HCPV systems are usually employed in small-scale installations, such as rooftops or remote areas with limited space.</p></p>
