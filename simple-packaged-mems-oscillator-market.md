<p><p>The Simple Packaged MEMS Oscillator market consists of two market types: Surface-Mount Device Package and Chip-Scale Package. </p><p>Surface-Mount Device Package refers to the packaging of MEMS oscillators into standard surface-mount device packages. These packages are designed to be mounted directly onto printed circuit boards, making them suitable for mass production and automated assembly processes.</p><p>On the other hand, Chip-Scale Package refers to the packaging of MEMS oscillators with minimal external dimensions. These packages are much smaller in size and are designed to be integrated directly onto the circuit board, offering space-saving benefits and enabling compact designs for various electronic devices.</p></p>
