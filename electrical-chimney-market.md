<p><p>An electrical chimney, also known as an electric range hood or electric exhaust fan, is a kitchen appliance that is used to remove smoke, fumes, and odors generated during cooking. It consists of a motorized fan that draws in air and a filter system that traps grease and other particles before releasing the filtered air back into the kitchen.</p><p>The electrical chimney market has been growing steadily in recent years. This growth can be attributed to the increasing awareness among consumers about the importance of maintaining good air quality in the kitchen, as well as the rising popularity of open-concept kitchens that require efficient ventilation systems. Additionally, the rapid growth of the food service industry and the increasing number of restaurants and cafes have also contributed to the demand for electrical chimneys.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the electrical chimney market is the integration of advanced technologies such as touch controls and smart sensors. These features enable users to control the chimney's functions more conveniently and efficiently. Furthermore, many electrical chimneys now come with energy-efficient LED lighting options that provide better visibility while cooking.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the growing popularity of wall-mounted electrical chimneys compared to traditional under-cabinet models. Wall-mounted chimneys offer better aesthetic appeal and are easier to clean and maintain.</p><p>According to market analysis, the electrical chimney market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to factors such as increasing disposable incomes, changing lifestyles, and a growing emphasis on health and hygiene.</p><p>Overall, the electrical chimney market is witnessing steady growth, driven by consumer demand for efficient and convenient ventilation solutions in their kitchens. The integration of advanced technologies and the move towards wall-mounted designs are some of the key trends shaping the market.</p></p>
