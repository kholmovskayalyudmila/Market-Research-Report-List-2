<p><p>Large scale battery storage refers to the storage of electricity generated from renewable energy sources such as solar and wind power on a large scale. It involves the use of advanced battery technologies to store excess power during periods of low demand and release it during peak demand periods, thereby balancing the supply and demand of electricity. Large scale battery storage systems play a crucial role in the integration of intermittent renewable energy sources into the grid, ensuring a stable and reliable power supply.</p><p>The global large scale battery storage market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is projected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period. Factors driving market growth include the increasing deployment of renewable energy sources, declining battery costs, and supportive government policies and incentives. One of the key trends is the development of advanced battery technologies with improved energy storage capacity and efficiency. Lithium-ion batteries dominate the large scale battery storage market; however, emerging technologies such as flow batteries, sodium sulfur batteries, and solid-state batteries are gaining traction due to their potential for higher energy density and longer lifespan.</p><p>Another trend is the integration of battery storage with renewable energy projects. This integration allows for better grid management and reduces curtailment of renewable power.
