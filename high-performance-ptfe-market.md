<p><p>The High Performance PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) market can be classified into three types: Granular, Aqueous Dispersion, and Micronized. Granular PTFE refers to solid particles with varying sizes used in molding and extrusion processes. Aqueous Dispersion PTFE is a water-based form used for coatings, where particles are suspended in water. Micronized PTFE is a fine powder with very small particle sizes, mainly used in lubricants and inks. These different types cater to various applications and require specific manufacturing processes to meet different customer requirements.</p></p>
