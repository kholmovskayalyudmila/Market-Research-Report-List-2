<p><strong>The Coordinate Measurement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bridge CMM</li><li>Horizontal CMM</li><li>Articulated-Arm CMM</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Coordinate Measurement System market is divided into several types. Bridge CMM, also known as gantry CMM, uses a bridge-like structure that allows the measurement probe to move along the X, Y, and Z axis. Horizontal CMM, on the other hand, has a fixed bridge with the measurement probe moving horizontally. Articulated-Arm CMM utilizes a flexible arm with rotary joints, providing high flexibility for measurements in confined spaces. Other types include optical CMMs, white light scanners, and laser trackers, which use non-contact techniques for measurement. These systems are widely used in manufacturing and quality control processes.</p></p>
