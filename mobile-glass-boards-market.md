<p><p>In the mobile glass boards market, there are two main types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic glass boards have a special surface that allows for the attachment of magnetic accessories such as markers or notes. This feature enables users to easily display and organize materials. On the other hand, non-magnetic glass boards lack this magnetic property and do not allow for the use of magnetic attachments. These boards are primarily used for writing and displaying information without needing additional magnetic accessories.</p></p>
