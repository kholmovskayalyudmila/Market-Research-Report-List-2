<p><p>The OTP display card market consists of two main types: OTP magnetic cards and OTP chip cards. OTP magnetic cards are embedded with a magnetic strip that stores the one-time password information. When users need to generate a password, they swipe the card on a designated device. Conversely, OTP chip cards contain a computer chip that securely stores the password data. Users can generate passwords by inserting the card into a compatible reader. Both types provide enhanced security and convenience compared to traditional password methods.</p></p>
