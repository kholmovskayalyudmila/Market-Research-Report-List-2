On the other hand, plastic Pasteur pipettes are made from Polyethylene (PE) or Polystyrene (PS) materials. They are lightweight, disposable, and offer convenience in various applications, such as in medical, pharmaceutical, and food industries. Both types serve the purpose of transferring small volumes of liquids accurately and safely.</p></p>
