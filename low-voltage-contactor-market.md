It is commonly used in residential and commercial applications for controlling motors, heating systems, and lighting. On the other hand, a DC contactor is specifically designed for direct current circuits, generally up to 1,500 volts. It is commonly used in industrial settings for controlling heavy-duty machinery, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems. Both types of contactors play a crucial role in electrical control systems.</p></p>
